## üîó **LINK BUILDING STRATEGY (What Are Backlinks?)**

### **What Are Backlinks? (Simple Explanation)**

**Backlinks = Other websites linking to your website**

Think of it like this:
- If lots of reputable websites link to you = Google thinks you're trustworthy
- More quality backlinks = Higher Google rankings
- It's like getting recommendations - the more people recommend you, the more trusted you become

**Example:**
- A local news site writes about your calculator and links to your site = 1 backlink
- An installer puts "Get quotes from our partners" and links to you = 1 backlink
- A directory lists your business with a link = 1 backlink

**Important:** Quality matters more than quantity. 10 good backlinks > 100 spammy ones.
