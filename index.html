Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar panel installation

How much do solar panels cost? +

Solar panel costs typically range from ¬£4,000 to ¬£8,000 for a complete system. The exact cost depends on your energy needs and roof size. We offer flexible financing options and government grants may be available.

How long does installation take? +

Most installations are completed in 1-2 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine. The entire process from consultation to activation typically takes 2-4 weeks.

What warranty do you provide? +

We provide a comprehensive 25-year warranty on all solar panels and components. This includes performance guarantees and covers both parts and labor for complete peace of mind.

Do I need planning permission? +

In most cases, solar panels fall under permitted development and don't require planning permission. However, we'll check your specific circumstances and handle any necessary applications.
