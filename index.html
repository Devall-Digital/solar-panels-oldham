Our calculator shows your specific payback period based on your energy usage, roof conditions, and location.

Do I need planning permission for solar panels in Oldham?

Most residential solar panel installations in Oldham and Greater Manchester don't require planning permission, as they fall under permitted development rights. However, there are exceptions for listed buildings, conservation areas, or if panels protrude more than 200mm from the roof. Always check with your local council or ask your installer for advice on your specific property.
