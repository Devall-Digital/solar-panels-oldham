1

Use Our Free Calculator

Start by using our interactive solar panel calculator to estimate your potential savings. Enter your details:

  • Your location (Oldham, Rochdale, Manchester, etc.)

  • Roof pitch and shading levels

Our calculator uses realistic UK solar panel data to give you accurate estimates of:

2

Request Free Quotes

Once you've calculated your potential savings, fill out our simple quote request form. We'll need:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Your address
  • Your calculator results (automatically included)

What happens next?

We'll connect you with qualified solar panel installers in your area. These installers are:

  • ✓ Fully qualified and certified
  • ✓ Insured and registered
  • ✓ Experienced in your area
  • ✓ Committed to competitive pricing

Installers will contact you directly with personalised quotes based on your property and requirements.

3

Compare and Choose

You'll receive quotes from multiple installers, allowing you to:

  • Compare prices - See different pricing options
  • Compare warranties - Check what's covered and for how long
  • Compare service - Evaluate installation timelines and customer service
  • Check reviews - Research each installer's reputation

No obligation - All quotes are free and there's no obligation to proceed. Take your time to compare and choose the installer that best meets your needs.

Direct contact - You'll deal directly with the installer. We simply facilitate the connection - you're in control.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get quotes?

Most installers will contact you within 24-48 hours of your request. Some may respond the same day.

Is there a cost to use your service?

No, our service is completely free. There are no charges for using our calculator or requesting quotes.

Am I obligated to use one of the installers?

No, there's no obligation. You're free to compare quotes and choose whichever installer you prefer, or none at all.

How do you choose which installers to work with?

We only work with installers who are:

  • Fully qualified and MCS certified
  • Properly insured
  • Experienced in solar panel installation
  • Committed to providing competitive pricing
  • Have positive customer reviews

Will the quotes match my calculator estimate?

Our calculator provides estimates based on typical UK solar panel costs and performance. Actual quotes may vary based on:

  • Specific property requirements
  • Roof access and complexity
  • Current market prices
  • Installer's specific pricing

However, our calculator gives you a good baseline to compare against.

What areas do you cover?

We connect homeowners with installers throughout Greater Manchester, including Oldham, Rochdale, Manchester, Ashton-under-Lyne, Bury, Bolton, and surrounding areas.
